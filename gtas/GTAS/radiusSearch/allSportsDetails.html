International Armwrestling Federation Georgia State Open Championships 2013

Weight Classes & Fees Free T-Shirts to first 50 Registrations; Awards or Trophies to top 3 in every class. King of the table match is open to all right hand competitors.

Clinic This is an after pull clinic with free goodie bag and informational handouts. The cost for the clinic is $5.00 paid at Registration.

Awards & Information Come and enjoy both the State of Georgia Arm Wrestling Championships for 2013 and also the Tactical Strength Challenge. Both occurring at the same venue. There will be vendors and lots of food and prizes.

All Non-Novice classes 1st Place Prize Money: over 3 = $50.00; over 7 = $100.00
